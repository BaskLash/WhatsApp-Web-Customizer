What is collected

  • Which customization features you trigger (event name + categorical metadata)
  • Counts (e.g. number of quick replies you have)
  • Extension version and your browser language
  • The anonymous ID above

What is NEVER collected

  • Messages, conversation content, or anything you type in WhatsApp
  • Contact names, phone numbers, or profile pictures
  • Group names, group descriptions, or member lists
  • Search queries or chat titles
